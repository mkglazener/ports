A Day in the Life

Monday

Mondays start the week. Depending on what projects I have, I typically work late on Mondays and then hit the rock-climbing gym after work.

Tuesday

Early mornings. Bike ride. Work. Hang with the roomie and watch TV.

Wednesday

Early mornings, bike rides to work. Work with the digstudio team. Then small group in the evening. Twice a month we do trivia.

Thursday

Thursday's are a little bit different as I wake up at 545 for a run. Head into work as I learn new projects and skills. Then I end the day usually with a happy hour or work activity.

Friday

Friday mornings are pretty typical and then I head to work, before usually grabbing dinner with friends.

The Weekend

Saturday

Depending on the week, Saturday's start off at the farmers market and weekly calls back home. Then whatever activity on the schedule for that weekend.

Sunday

Church. Friends. Rockclimbing. Meal Prep.
